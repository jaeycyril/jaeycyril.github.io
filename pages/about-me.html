About Me

Hello, World!

My name is ChukwuDumebi.

I randomly decided to study Computer Science and have never looked back since. My initial focus was Software Engineering but the areas which I spend most of my days include Data Engineering, Machine Learning and DevOps/MLOps. I have taken an interest in Cloud Computing and I am currently an AWS certified developer.

Every developer knows about the utility of Stack Overflow as a solution repo but the recent explosion of articles and blogs have also made learning a much more exciting experience. There is a high probability of finding an article on a useful subject by an author who speaks the language which you understand. That is why I decided to start writing as well. To contribute my own quota to that knowledge base.

I intend to write about tools and libraries that I use and enjoy. I also want to reflect on some of my experiences. I have heard that writing is an amazing medium to channel incongruent thoughts, so I guess this blog would be very useful for me and hopefully, you.

Outside tech, I enjoy playing football, reading fiction and non-fiction books and cooking.

Please enjoy and feel free to hit me up on social media.
